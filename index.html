ABOUT US

Welcome to The Royal Thet, where the elegance of a luxury cafe meets the warmth of Himalayan hospitality. As a trader and BSc CSIT student from Nepal, I've curated a haven that blends exceptional coffee, exquisite cuisine, and seamless reservations. Join us for a sensory journey, where every sip and bite is a celebration of the extraordinary. The Royal Thet - Where Opulence Meets Himalayan Charm.
